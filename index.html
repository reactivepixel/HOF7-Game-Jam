Game Jam for Cerebral Palsy

The Gaming Industry recognizes the need to be inclusive of gamers with disabilities and physical and cognitive difficulties. Full Sail is proud to help advance this awareness by working with the Conductive Education Center of Orlando (CECO) to challenge five teams to develop a game within 48 hours targeted at Students at CECO with Cerebral Palsy and other motor disabilities.
